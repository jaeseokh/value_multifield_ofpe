2 Overview

In this chapter I give a high-level overview of the project using seven figures:

  1. The spatial distribution of all OFPE fields used in this study (ambiguous US map).
  2. An example showing the limitations of tree-based ensemble models for yield prediction at a field with multiple years of trials.
  3. Yield–N response curves for the same field, comparing ex-post “true” responses to machine learning forecasts.
  4. The Illinois-only subset of fields used in the APSIM–Bayesian prototype.
  5. APSIM-based prior yield–N distributions for representative Illinois soils under long-term historical weather.
  6. A conceptual illustration of how OFPE data update APSIM priors.
  7. An example posterior yield–N curve for an Illinois field after Bayesian updating.

Technical details, full code, and additional figures appear in later chapters.
